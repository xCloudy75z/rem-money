How to choose

  1. Scan the 60-pixel column first. If the mark survives there, it survives everywhere.
  2. Cross-reference against the dark wallpaper. iOS users skew dark; the mark must hold its own on black.
  3. Ignore the accent column when judging — the icon usually sits on the user's wallpaper, not on brand color.
  4. Pick the one you'd recognize in your peripheral vision.

Then tell me

Reply with the letter — A · B · C · D · E · F · or +Hybrid. I'll ship it as the icon, generate the PNG fallbacks (180 / 192 / 512), wire the manifest, and re-deploy.
